Pythagoras of Samos

Pythagoras of Samos is an ancient Greek philosopher, mathematician, music theorist and mystic, the creator of the religious and philosophical school of the Pythagoreans.His name is associated with the Pythagorean theorem, one of the most famous and important theorems in geometry. Pythagoras and his school had a significant influence on the development of science and philosophy in ancient Greece and had a long-term impact on European culture and mathematics. His theorem remains a core topic in the study of geometry and mathematics and serves as an important tool for solving a variety of problems in science and engineering.
